Why buy one tool when you can buy three? With the HART 20V 3-Tool Combo Kit, you get a ½” Drill/Driver, an Impact Driver, a Circular Saw, (2) 2.0Ah Lithium-ion Batteries and a Fast Charger. Whether this kit expands your HART lineup or this is your first time, you’re in for a nice surprise. To start, all 20V power tools work with ALL HART 20V batteries. They’re also cordless, so you can work wherever you'd like. And HART backs it up with a limited 3-year warranty on all tools, batteries and chargers. With the included ½” Drill/Driver, you get a 2-speed gearbox for optimizing speed and control—and a 24-position clutch for adjusting torque while driving fasteners. The Impact Driver offers high-speed fastening with up to 1,700 in-lbs. of torque for demanding applications and materials. And the Circular Saw provides high-speed cutting with 4,700 RPM and a 0°-56° bevel for more angled cut options.
